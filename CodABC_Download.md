## Information ##

**CodABC (current version 2.0.0)**

CodABC is an evolutionary framework to coestimate recombination, substitution and molecular adaptation (nonsynonymous synonymous rates ratio, dN/dS or Ï‰) rates by approximate Bayesian computation from coding sequence alignments. CodABC is freely available from http://code.google.com/p/codabc/
